Context

Project Overview

The Advertising Club Bangalore is one of India's most respected advertising industry bodies. The current website doesn't reflect the quality of the community behind it, or the calibre of events it runs.

This engagement is a full redesign. Every page, every flow, every member state, reimagined from the ground up. Design only, no development. We design it, you build it.

The goal is simple. A website that earns the trust of members, students, and the wider industry, the moment they land on it.

Research & Information Gathering

Secondary research and competitive benchmarking across advertising clubs, industry associations, and community-driven membership websites. We map what works and what doesn't, to identify design directions, interaction patterns, and clear opportunities for differentiation.
