Small mono index chip (DAY 1, 01) — a 2px box that numbers agenda items, phases and day blocks.

```jsx
<NumTag>DAY 1</NumTag>
<NumTag variant="accent">H1</NumTag>
<NumTag variant="ghost">01</NumTag>
```

- `variant`: `ink` (solid black, default) · `accent` (orange) · `ghost` (hairline).
- Mono, uppercase, 2px radius — never a pill. Pair with a serif title beside it.
