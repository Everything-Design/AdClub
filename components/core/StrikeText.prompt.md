Inline emphasis by color — a yellow marker strike or accent-orange text. The system never uses italics for emphasis.

```jsx
<p>so KaarTech and Everything Design can <StrikeText>decide together</StrikeText></p>
<p>A proposal you can <StrikeText variant="accent">act on.</StrikeText></p>
```

- `variant`: `strike` (yellow highlight, default) · `accent` (orange text).
- Use the yellow strike at most once per page. Reserve it for the single most important phrase.
