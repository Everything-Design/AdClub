Warm-black feature block — the one place ink becomes a surface. Accent kicker, paper serif title, warm-grey body. Use for sign-off cards, estimate bands, the closing CTA.

```jsx
<DarkPanel label="The commitment" title="Nine working days.">
  <p style={{ color: "var(--on-ink-soft)" }}>Two weeks, senior-led, fixed.</p>
</DarkPanel>
```

- Body text inside should use `var(--on-ink-soft)` (warm grey). Keep one accent kicker.
- One dark panel per page at most — it is the visual full stop.
